MY EDUCATION

My primary school life begun when I was 7 years old. The school is Sekolah Kebangsaan Bandar Baru Sungai Lalang Kedah. I pass with flying colors in UPSR in 5A.
Then I went to secondary school, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Agama Sik. I managed to do well in PT3 in Form 3 and continued my Form 4 in the same school.
Once again I did quite well in SPM and I have been offered to enter Sijil Tinggi Pelajaran Malaysia, STPM in 2018. I completed my study in 2 years and have been
accepted to further my first degree in UiTM Machang, Kelantan. Now, I'm a second year student in Bachelor of Administration.
